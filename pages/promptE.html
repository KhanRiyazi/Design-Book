Prompt Techniques

Zero-Shot Prompting: 
"Translate the following English text to French: 'Hello, how are you?'"
Few-Shot Prompting: 
"Apple -> Manzana, Banana -> Plátano, Orange -> Naranja"
Chain-of-Thought: 
"Let's think step by step. If I have 5 apples and eat 2, how many do I have left?"
